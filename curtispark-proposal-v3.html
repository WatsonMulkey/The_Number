Our Approach
A Site That Works for You

The Curtis Park website should be a hub that supports the ambitions and needs of the board and the residents of Curtis Park. Updating the site so it's easily usable by all community members — and easily maintained by members of the board — is the main goal.

FOIL will improve the things that need improving (accessibility for screen readers, navigation, visual consistency), make suggestions and execute on potential upgrades, and make sure there's documentation and clear instructions so the board can maintain the website on their own. I'll also provide several post-engagement sessions for new improvements, questions, or QA check-ins.

The site is in good shape overall, and this is a smart time to address a few concerns while they're still small. More importantly, making sure the board has the ability and the confidence to make changes as needed is a top priority.

What We'll Focus On

Keep What Works

The historic papers, event calendar, payment processing, and self-service content updates are all important — they stay, and they get better.

Ease of Use

Every neighbor who visits the site should be able to find what they need without guesswork. Clear navigation and functionality, and a simple layout.

Accessibility

Adding alt text to images, improving labels, and making the site work well with screen readers so it's usable by everyone in the community.

Look & Feel

Visual updates, better navigation, and smarter feature placement — so the site feels current and the most important things are easy to find.
