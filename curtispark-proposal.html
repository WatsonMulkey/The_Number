Project Timeline
How We'll Work Together
1
Interview & Consultation
We sit down together and walk through the site. What's working, what's frustrating, what's missing. We'll define priorities, review the audit findings, and agree on exactly what "done" looks like — before any work begins.
2
Build & Collaborate
Design and development with regular check-ins. You'll see progress throughout — not just at the end. We'll work through content migration, calendar setup, donation integration, and the volunteer board together. Questions and feedback happen in real time, not in a big reveal.
3
Review & Refine
Full walkthrough of the finished site. We test everything: accessibility compliance, mobile responsiveness, donation flow, calendar management, content editing. Any adjustments happen here before launch.
4
Handover & Training
Your team gets hands-on training for managing content, posting events, uploading the Curtis Park Times, and managing volunteer listings. You'll have documentation and direct access to me for questions after launch. The goal: you don't need me after this.
