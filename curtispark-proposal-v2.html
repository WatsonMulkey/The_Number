Based on my understanding of your need, this can be a relatively straightforward project. Making sure the Curtis Park neighborhood site works well for you and the residents of Curtis Park doesn't necessarily mean a lot of flash or style.

Updating the site to improve its accessibility for visitors who use screen readers, making sure the event calendar is functional and easy to update, and providing the board with the tools and resources they need to easily make updates and changes are our big priorities.

Inclusive & Accessible

Accessibility & Content

  • Fix duplicate card copy (Maintaining Historic Homes)
  • Fix broken image link (Resources card)
  • Fix non-functional Learn More button
  • Clean up empty heading tags
  • Fix contact form labeling
Discoverable Online

Search & Analytics

  • Replace boilerplate meta description with real content

  • Consistent card layouts & visual cleanup
