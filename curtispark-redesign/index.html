Our Community

Bringing neighbors together since 1971

Curtis Park Neighbors is a Registered Neighborhood Organization (RNO) serving one of Denver's most historic communities. We encourage participation in the life of the neighborhood through committees, events, and civic engagement.

From tree planting to design review, from preserving flagstone sidewalks to improving Sonny Lawson Park — we work together to make Curtis Park extraordinary.

  • Foundation repairs and re-roofing
  • Brick masonry repointing
